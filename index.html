A Spotify Song Suggesterthat helps users find and visualize songs that fit their personal taste.

This app enables users to browse and visualize audio features of over 116k Spotify songs. The app identifies songs with similar audio features. Users can save their favorite songs to their profile and the app will make suggestions based on the audio features of their favorites. Users can also request suggested songs based on what they are in the mood for.
